496 Fairlawn Avenue

Property Description

Welcome to 496 Fairlawn Avenue, nestled in the serene neighbourhood of Bedford Park. This elegant four-plus-one-bedroom, six-bathroom home boasts a tasteful blend of timeless and contemporary styles. It is ideal for those looking to enjoy family time with their loved ones while having luxury lifestyle amenities at their fingertips. Walk to the shops and restaurants at Avenue and Lawrence. Enjoy close proximity to the top public and private schools.

Grand entryway with sleek porcelain floors and a stunning chandelier welcomes you into the home. The spacious living areas on the main level feature rich hardwood floors, 10′ ceilings, and refined millwork, creating a perfect backdrop for both intimate gatherings and grand entertaining.

The heart of the home is the generous open-concept chef’s kitchen, equipped with Wolf and Sub-Zero appliances, quartz countertops, an oversized centre island, custom cabinetry, butler’s pantry, and a breakfast nook overlooking the back garden oasis. Seamlessly transition to the family room with two hinged French doors offers an abundance of natural light and gorgeous views of the ultra-private lush outdoors.

Upstairs, the airy second floor features a skylight, high ceilings, and hardwood floors throughout. Escape every day in the primary suite retreat with a seven-piece spa bathroom, steam shower and two walk-in closets. The three other bedrooms are each spacious and offer their own walk-in closets plus ensuite bathrooms, perfect for kids and guests.

The lower level is completely finished and offers a spectacular space for a recreation room with radiant heated floors throughout, gas fireplace, built-in projector, an additional guest or nanny suite and a wet bar make it perfect for entertaining.

Completing this home is a beautiful professionally landscaped back garden with a newly-lined pristine saltwater pool, hot tub, and flagstone deck, providing a stunning summer getaway in the city. Built by Madison Homes, 496 Fairlawn is a harmonious blend of sophistication and comfort, presenting an exceptional living experience.
